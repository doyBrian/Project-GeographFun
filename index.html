This is an educational tool for kids to learn more about other countries in the world!

A note to parents and educators using our app:

This is a safe environment for your kids. No important information will be required or saved of any user.

We only ask for their first name and state they live in to personalize their experience while using our tool.

All information provided by the app are filtered so it serves the purpose of this tool.

A note to users:

Simply Double click on countries you want to explore.

Learn Facts about any country and have fun!

Happy Exploring!
